Important note about data collection

This is part of a research project. We don’t collect any data from you. We don’t have any data about your personality traits. The only data we collect is, how many people clicked on our facebook ad. And also this data is completely anonymous! All we want is to raise awareness to your digital footprint and how it could be used. We don’t know what your Facebook interests and likes are, only Facebook has this information. And with this information Facebook can show ads to specific target groups with similar interests.
