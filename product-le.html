The Observant One

The Observant One tends to be considered cautious, reserved and serious. They can seem distant and withdrawn. Especially in new situations, surrounded by new people, they observe the situation first before revealing too much of themselves. They simply prefer doing things by themselves but not because they lack social competence, they rather enjoy their independence and spending their time by themselves. On top of that, The Observant One is a great observer, they absorb situations carefully before actively participating. They avoid saying too much unnecessarily and only express themselves when they think it is important.

All this can make them seem very wise and you will most likely hear great advice from them. On the other hand, because of their preference for being alone, they can be misjudged as unhappy, although they just often don’t feel the need for company. The Observant One avoids expressing their emotions openly, they rather keep a lot of things to themselves, which can make them appear unapproachable and distant. You’ll probably also find yourself struggling to keep up small talk with The Observant One .
